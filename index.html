About Us

Who We Are

Leather Electrical is a PNG-owned electrical contracting company established in 2011. We specialize in Low Voltage (LV) and Medium Voltage (MV) electrical installations, industrial maintenance, commissioning, and electrical construction.


Our experienced team has successfully completed projects for hospitals, government agencies, industrial facilities, and commercial businesses throughout Papua New Guinea.


We are committed to delivering safe, reliable, and professional electrical solutions that meet industry standards and client expectations.
