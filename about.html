About Leather Electrical

Leather Electrical is a trusted Papua New Guinea engineering company specializing in electrical systems, telecommunications infrastructure, generator installation, and industrial maintenance solutions across PNG.

Who We Are

Engineering Excellence Across Papua New Guinea

Leather Electrical has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality electrical and telecommunications solutions throughout Papua New Guinea. With years of industry experience, our team has successfully completed projects in telecommunications, industrial electrical systems, power installations, and infrastructure support.

Our company works closely with businesses, contractors, government organizations, and telecommunications providers to ensure every project is delivered safely, professionally, and on schedule.

We are committed to innovation, safety, technical excellence, and reliable customer service in every project we undertake.
